About Me

I am a Full-Stack Developer specializing in enterprise-level web applications, with expertise in Java 17, Spring Boot, React, and PostgreSQL. Currently, I'm developing a comprehensive Swedish Media Annotation Platform at SVT, featuring a microservices architecture, complex database relationships, and a modern React/TypeScript frontend with advanced analytics dashboards.

My experience spans the entire full-stack development lifecycle — from database design and RESTful API architecture to modern frontend development with React, TypeScript, and data visualization. I focus on building scalable, maintainable solutions using Docker containerization, implementing complex business logic, and designing intuitive user interfaces for data-intensive applications.

I thrive in agile environments, collaborating with cross-functional teams to deliver high-quality software. My recent work demonstrates strong expertise in media content management, speech recognition workflows, and analytics platforms with real-time data processing. I'm also experienced with Kubernetes and k9s for container orchestration, as well as Python for automation and data processing. For UI/UX collaboration, I frequently use Figma.

As a certified Scrum Master, I have extensive experience facilitating sprint planning, retrospectives, and promoting effective team collaboration. I'm proficient with modern communication and project management tools such as Teams, Slack, Trello, GitHub, GitLab, and Mural for remote collaboration and visual planning. I also have hands-on experience implementing CI/CD pipelines for automated testing and deployment.

My strength lies in my ability to prioritize tasks and adapt to change. I'm known for being a team player and well-structured person. While I can get stressed when facing tight deadlines, I continuously work on improving by tackling challenges one at a time. This approach has helped me deliver successful projects and maintain high-quality codebases.
